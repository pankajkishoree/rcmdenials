Insurance Payer Directory: Phone Numbers, IVR Shortcuts, and Contact Info

Finding the right phone number and navigating an insurance company's phone tree can waste 20-30 minutes per call. Our Payer Directory consolidates contact information for 30+ insurance payers, including direct department numbers, IVR navigation shortcuts, fax numbers, and mailing addresses — all in one searchable directory.

What's Included for Each Payer

For every payer in the directory, you get complete contact information organized by department: claims status, provider enrollment, appeals and grievances, prior authorization, eligibility verification, and member services. Each department includes the main phone number, IVR navigation steps to reach a live representative, fax numbers for document submission, and mailing addresses for written correspondence.

IVR Navigation Shortcuts

Insurance phone trees are designed to deflect calls, not connect you to the right person. Our IVR shortcuts show you the exact按键 sequences to bypass automated menus and reach live representatives. These shortcuts are updated regularly as payers change their phone systems — saving your team from memorizing new sequences every time a payer updates their IVR.

Departments Covered

  • Claims processing — Status inquiries, payment questions, and claim adjustments
  • Provider relations — Credentialing, enrollment, and contract questions
  • Appeals and grievances — Denial appeals, complaint filing, and escalation paths
  • Prior authorization — Pre-approval requirements and submission processes
  • Eligibility — Patient coverage verification and benefits questions
  • Fraud and abuse — Reporting and compliance contacts

Why This Directory Saves Time

AR teams make 50-100 payer calls per week. Even saving 5 minutes per call adds up to hours of recovered productivity each month. The directory eliminates the "hold and transfer" cycle by providing direct department numbers and IVR shortcuts that get your team to the right person on the first try.

Frequently Asked Questions

Which insurance payers are included in the directory?
The directory covers 30+ major payers including UnitedHealthcare, Aetna, Cigna, Humana, Blue Cross Blue Shield, Medicare (CMS), Medicaid (state-specific), Kaiser Permanente, Centene, Molina, and many more. New payers are added regularly.
How often is the contact information updated?
We review and update payer contact information quarterly, with urgent updates made within 2 weeks when payers change their phone systems or department numbers. IVR shortcuts are verified monthly to ensure accuracy.
Can I search by payer name or state?
Yes. The directory supports search by payer name, abbreviation, or state. You can also filter by plan type (HMO, PPO, EPO, POS) to find the correct contact information for specific plan configurations.
Does this include Medicare and Medicaid contacts?
Yes. The directory includes CMS/Medicare contacts for all Administrative QIOs and MACs, plus Medicaid contacts for all 50 states. State-specific Medicaid programs have their own entries with the correct state agency contacts.
Is this directory free to use?
Yes. The Payer Directory is completely free with no usage limits. Bookmark the page for quick access during your AR calls, or copy numbers directly to your phone or CRM system.
