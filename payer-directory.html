Insurance Payer Contact Directory with IVR Navigation

Locating the correct department and navigating insurance phone trees consumes 20-30 minutes per payer call. RCM Denials' Payer Directory consolidates contact information for 30+ insurance payers, including direct department numbers, IVR navigation shortcuts, fax numbers, and mailing addresses — eliminating the hold-and-transfer cycle that drains AR team productivity.

Department-Level Contact Information

Each payer entry includes contact details organized by functional department: claims status, provider enrollment, appeals and grievances, prior authorization, eligibility verification, and member services. Every department entry provides the direct phone number, IVR key sequence to reach a live representative, fax number for document submission, and mailing address for written correspondence.

IVR Shortcut Database

Insurance phone trees are designed to deflect calls, not route them efficiently. The directory's IVR shortcuts document the exact key sequences to bypass automated menus and reach live representatives. Shortcuts are verified monthly and updated within two weeks of payer phone system changes.

Departments Indexed

  • Claims processing — Status inquiries, payment reconciliation, and claim adjustment requests
  • Provider relations — Credentialing, enrollment, and contract administration
  • Appeals and grievances — Denial appeals, formal complaint filing, and escalation procedures
  • Prior authorization — Pre-approval requirements, submission processes, and status checks
  • Eligibility verification — Patient coverage confirmation and benefits inquiry
  • Compliance contacts — Fraud reporting, abuse hotlines, and regulatory compliance channels

Productivity Impact

AR teams averaging 50-100 payer calls per week recover significant productive time by eliminating phone tree navigation. Direct department numbers and IVR shortcuts reduce average call connection time from 8-12 minutes to under 2 minutes, translating to hours of recovered capacity monthly across the team.

Frequently Asked Questions

Which insurance payers are included in the directory?
The directory covers 30+ major payers including UnitedHealthcare, Aetna, Cigna, Humana, Blue Cross Blue Shield, Medicare (CMS), Medicaid (state-specific), Kaiser Permanente, Centene, Molina, and many more. New payers are added regularly.
How often is the contact information updated?
We review and update payer contact information quarterly, with urgent updates made within 2 weeks when payers change their phone systems or department numbers. IVR shortcuts are verified monthly to ensure accuracy.
Can I search by payer name or state?
Yes. The directory supports search by payer name, abbreviation, or state. You can also filter by plan type (HMO, PPO, EPO, POS) to find the correct contact information for specific plan configurations.
Does this include Medicare and Medicaid contacts?
Yes. The directory includes CMS/Medicare contacts for all Administrative QIOs and MACs, plus Medicaid contacts for all 50 states. State-specific Medicaid programs have their own entries with the correct state agency contacts.
Is this directory free to use?
Yes. The Payer Directory is completely free with no usage limits. Bookmark the page for quick access during your AR calls, or copy numbers directly to your phone or CRM system.
