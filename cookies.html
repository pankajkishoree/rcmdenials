Cookie Policy

Last updated: June 25, 2026

1. Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how RCM Denials ("we," "our," or "us") uses cookies and similar local storage technologies—including browser local storage (localStorage) and local databases (IndexedDB)—when you visit and use our Platform.

2. What Technologies We Use

To provide a premium, privacy-first user experience without storing any of your patient or claim data on central cloud servers, RCM Denials relies heavily on local browser storage technologies:

  • localStorage: Used to save UI states (such as active theme mode, sidebar toggles, search preferences, and onboarding configurations).
  • IndexedDB: A lightweight, high-performance database built into your browser. We use it to save and manage active call sessions, batch claims logs, timely filing calculations history, and call analytics metrics.
  • Cookies: Small text files placed on your device to maintain basic, anonymous routing details or support privacy-respecting website security rules.

3. Why We Use Local Storage

These storage methods are essential for:

  • Functionality: Allowing you to exit the tab and return later without losing active call checklists, call timers, or billing note progress.
  • Data Protection: Keeping clinical AR calling information strictly on your local device. Because the data remains in IndexedDB/localStorage, it never crosses the internet to our servers.
  • Preferences: Keeping your chosen UI settings (e.g., Dark Theme vs Light Theme) saved across multiple visits.

4. Third-Party Analytics

We may occasionally use privacy-friendly third-party analytics services to help us understand Platform usage patterns, optimize performance, and identify bugs. These services may place basic analytical cookies or collect anonymous browser statistics (such as screen size or device operating system), but they never collect patient details, claim notes, or other clinical inputs.

5. Managing and Clearing Your Data

Since your data is entirely controlled by your browser, you have complete power to manage, export, or clear it at any time:

  • You can use the "Export Session" or "Reset Data" buttons within the Platform to backup or wipe your database records.
  • You can clear your browser cache, cookies, and website data through your browser settings, which will instantly erase all active call sessions and histories.
  • You can configure your browser to block cookies or block local storage, though this will prevent RCM Denials from functioning properly.
