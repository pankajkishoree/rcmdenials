Cookie Policy

How we use cookies and local storage technologies

Home / Cookie Policy

Last Updated: June 28, 2026

1. Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how RCM Denials ("we," "our," or "us") uses cookies and similar local storage technologies — including browser localStorage and IndexedDB — when you visit and use our AI-powered medical billing platform at rcmdenials.com.

2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work efficiently, provide a better user experience, and supply information to site owners. In addition to traditional cookies, we use browser-based storage technologies that serve a similar purpose.

3. Technologies We Use

RCM Denials uses the following technologies:

3.1 Cookies

Small text files placed on your device for basic, anonymous functionality such as security rules and routing.

3.2 localStorage

A browser storage mechanism used to save UI states including theme preferences (dark/light mode), sidebar toggles, search preferences, and onboarding configurations. This data never leaves your device.

3.3 IndexedDB

A lightweight, high-performance database built into your browser. We use it to save and manage:

  • Active call sessions and checklists
  • Batch claims logs and history
  • Timely filing calculations
  • Claims tracker entries
  • Call analytics metrics

Privacy note: All data stored in localStorage and IndexedDB remains entirely on your device. It is never transmitted to our servers or any third party.

4. Types of Cookies We Use

4.1 Essential Cookies

Required for basic platform functionality. These cannot be disabled.

  • Theme preference — Remembers your dark/light mode selection
  • Session state — Maintains your active session across page reloads

4.2 Functional Cookies

Enhance your experience by remembering your preferences.

  • UI preferences — Sidebar state, search settings, onboarding completion
  • Display settings — Table view preferences, sort orders

4.3 Analytics Cookies

Used to understand how visitors interact with the platform. All data is anonymous.

  • Google Analytics — Anonymous traffic analytics (pages visited, session duration, device type)

5. Third-Party Cookies

The following third-party services may place cookies on your device:

5.1 Google Analytics

Google Analytics uses cookies to collect anonymous traffic data. This helps us understand how visitors use the platform so we can improve it. Google Analytics does not collect personal information. Learn more about Google's privacy practices.

5.2 Cloudflare

Cloudflare may place essential cookies for security, performance, and DDoS protection. Learn more about Cloudflare's privacy practices.

5.3 Google Fonts

Google Fonts may place cookies to deliver fonts efficiently. Learn more.

6. Cookie Duration

  • Session cookies — Deleted when you close your browser
  • Persistent cookies — Remain until they expire or you delete them
  • localStorage — Persists until you clear it or the platform removes it
  • IndexedDB — Persists until you clear it or the platform removes it

7. Managing Cookies

You have full control over cookies and local storage. You can:

  • Clear all data — Use your browser's settings to clear cookies, localStorage, and IndexedDB for rcmdenials.com
  • Block cookies — Configure your browser to block cookies (note: this may prevent the Platform from functioning properly)
  • Use incognito/private mode — Data is automatically cleared when you close the private window
  • Export data — Use the platform's built-in export feature to save your data before clearing

8. Browser-Specific Instructions

  • Chrome — Settings → Privacy and Security → Clear browsing data → Select "Cookies and other site data"
  • Firefox - Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data → Clear Data
  • Safari — Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data → Remove for rcmdenials.com
  • Edge — Settings → Privacy, search, and services → Clear browsing data

9. Impact of Disabling Cookies

If you block or clear cookies and local storage:

  • Your theme preference will not be saved
  • Active call sessions will be lost
  • Claims tracker data will be cleared
  • Timely filing calculation history will be erased

The core tools will still function, but your data will not persist between sessions.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of RCM Denials after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
