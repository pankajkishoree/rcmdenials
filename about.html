Who We Are

RCM Denials is a free, AI-powered medical billing and claim denial management platform built specifically for healthcare Accounts Receivable (AR) callers, medical billers, and revenue cycle management teams. We combine artificial intelligence with deep healthcare billing expertise to help professionals resolve claim denials faster, validate coding accuracy, and recover revenue that would otherwise be lost.

Our platform serves AR callers, medical billing specialists, RCM teams, hospital financial departments, and healthcare providers across the United States who deal with claim denials on a daily basis.

Our Mission

Healthcare revenue cycle teams lose billions of dollars every year to preventable claim denials — not because they lack skill, but because they lack real-time, intelligent support during the most critical moments. Our mission is to give every AR caller and medical biller an AI co-pilot that knows payer rules, denial codes, and escalation strategies, so nothing falls through the cracks.

We believe that every healthcare organization deserves access to professional-grade denial management tools, regardless of budget. That's why RCM Denials is — and always will be — completely free.

Why RCM Denials

Medical billing is complex. With thousands of denial codes, constantly changing payer rules, strict timely filing deadlines, and hundreds of insurance companies each with their own processes, AR callers need every advantage they can get. RCM Denials provides that advantage through a comprehensive suite of AI-powered tools designed to streamline every aspect of the denial resolution workflow.
