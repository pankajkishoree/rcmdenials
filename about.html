About RCM Denials

Last updated: June 25, 2026

What We Do

RCM Denials is an AI-powered Revenue Cycle Management assistant designed specifically for AR (Accounts Receivable) callers in the healthcare industry. We help billing professionals navigate complex payer calls in real time — reducing hold times, improving first-call resolution, and accelerating claim recovery.

Our platform provides intelligent checklists, decision trees, timely filing calculators, payer directories with IVR shortcuts, and an AI call assistant that guides you through every denial scenario.

Our Mission

Healthcare revenue cycle teams lose billions every year to preventable claim denials — not because they lack skill, but because they lack real-time, intelligent support during the most critical moments: payer phone calls. Our mission is to give every AR caller an AI co-pilot that knows payer rules, denial codes, and escalation strategies, so nothing falls through the cracks.

Key Features

  • AI Denial Checklist Generator — payer-specific and denial-specific question checklists for every call
  • Decision Tree Engine — guided next steps based on representative responses
  • Timely Filing Calculator — never miss a filing deadline again
  • Payer Directory & IVR Navigator — direct numbers and IVR shortcuts for major payers
  • Batch Claims Tracker — manage multiple claims in parallel
  • Post-Call Summary — one-click professional AR notes for your billing system
  • Analytics Dashboard — track productivity, recovery rates, and denial trends
  • AI Call Assistant — real-time guidance as you speak with payer representatives

Who It's For

RCM Denials is built for:

  • AR callers and billing specialists
  • Revenue cycle management teams
  • Medical billing companies
  • Hospital and clinic financial departments
  • Healthcare providers dealing with claim denials
