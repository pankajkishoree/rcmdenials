How AI Claim Risk Review Prevents Denials Before Submission

Claim denials cost the healthcare industry over $262 billion annually, with the average denial requiring $118 in rework to resolve. Our AI Claim Risk Review tool analyzes your claims before submission, identifying the specific denial risks that could cost your practice thousands of dollars in lost revenue.

What the Tool Analyzes

Our rule engine evaluates six critical dimensions of every claim: patient demographics and eligibility, insurance plan requirements, provider credentials and specialty matching, diagnosis-to-procedure medical necessity, documentation completeness, and prior authorization status. Each dimension is scored individually, then combined into an overall risk score from 0 to 100.

Common Denial Risks We Catch

  • Medical necessity mismatches — ICD-10 codes that don't support the billed CPT procedures
  • Missing modifiers — Required modifiers not included for specific payer contracts
  • Eligibility gaps — Patient insurance status issues that trigger automatic denials
  • Coordination of benefits errors — Primary and secondary payer sequencing mistakes
  • Prior authorization lapses — Procedures requiring pre-approval that weren't obtained
  • Billing frequency limits — Codes billed beyond allowed frequency thresholds

Who Should Use This Tool

Medical billers, coding specialists, practice managers, and revenue cycle teams use this tool to catch errors before claims leave the building. Whether you process 50 claims a day or 5,000, a single pre-submission review can save hours of post-denial rework. The tool works for all specialties and all payer types, including Medicare, Medicaid, and commercial plans.

How to Get the Best Results

Enter complete claim information across all six steps for the most accurate risk assessment. The more data you provide — including modifiers, place of service, and clinical documentation notes — the more precise the analysis. Use the detailed breakdown to prioritize which claims need additional documentation before submission.

Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the AI claim risk assessment?
Our rule engine uses CMS guidelines, CCI edits, and payer-specific rules to identify denial risks with high accuracy. The tool catches the most common denial reasons that account for over 80% of all claim denials in the healthcare industry.
Does this tool work for all insurance payers?
Yes. The tool analyzes claim elements against universal coding rules and medical necessity guidelines. It works for Medicare, Medicaid, and all major commercial payers including UnitedHealthcare, Aetna, Cigna, and Blue Cross Blue Shield.
Can I use this tool for high-volume claim processing?
Absolutely. The tool is designed for both individual claim reviews and batch processing workflows. Many billing teams use it as a final quality check before electronic claim submission to clearhouses.
What happens after I see the risk score?
The results page provides specific, actionable steps to fix each identified risk factor. You'll see recommended review actions, potential denial categories, and a confidence level indicating how certain the assessment is for each finding.
Is this tool free to use?
Yes. Our AI Claim Risk Review tool is completely free with no usage limits. You can review as many claims as needed without creating an account or providing payment information.
