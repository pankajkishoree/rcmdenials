CO-331

CO-331 Denial Code: Claim Denied

Last Updated: June 2026 | Category: Timely Filing

📋 What is CO-331 Denial Code?

CO-331 indicates that the claim has been denied because the appeal was not submitted within the required appeal timeframe.

🔍 Common Causes of CO-331 Denials

❌

Common Causes

  • Appeal deadline missed
  • Late appeal submission
  • No documentation of appeal timeframe
  • Appeal not filed timely

✅ How to Resolve CO-331 Denials

1

Review the Denial

Carefully review the remittance advice to understand the specific denial reason.

  • Check if the appeal deadline can be extended
  • Gather proof of timely appeal submission
  • If deadline was met, appeal the denial with proof
  • Request good cause exception if applicable

📞 What to Ask the Payer

Essential Questions for CO-331 Denials:

  1. "What specific documentation is needed to resolve this denial?"
  2. "What is the appeal deadline for this denial?"
  3. "Can this claim be corrected and resubmitted?"
  4. "Is there a retro-authorization or exception process available?"

💡 Prevention Strategies

  • Track all appeal deadlines in a calendar
  • Set up reminders for appeal submissions
  • Document all appeal submissions with proof
  • Train staff on appeal timeframe requirements
