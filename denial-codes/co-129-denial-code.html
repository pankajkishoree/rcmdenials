CO-129

CO-129 Denial Code: Payment Adjusted Due to

Last Updated: June 2026 | Category: Payment Adjustment

📋 What is CO-129 Denial Code?

CO-129 indicates that payment has been adjusted due to the amount being applied to the patient's cost-sharing obligations.

🔍 Common Causes of CO-129 Denials

❌

Common Causes

  • Patient deductible applied
  • Coinsurance applied
  • Copay applied
  • Cost-sharing arrangement in effect

✅ How to Resolve CO-129 Denials

1

Review the Denial

Carefully review the remittance advice to understand the specific denial reason.

  • Verify the correct patient responsibility amount
  • Confirm cost-sharing is correctly calculated
  • If adjustment is wrong, appeal with plan documents

📞 What to Ask the Payer

Essential Questions for CO-129 Denials:

  1. "What specific documentation is needed to resolve this denial?"
  2. "What is the appeal deadline for this denial?"
  3. "Can this claim be corrected and resubmitted?"
  4. "Is there a retro-authorization or exception process available?"

💡 Prevention Strategies

  • Verify patient cost-sharing before billing
  • Calculate deductible, coinsurance, and copay correctly
  • Track patient cost-sharing across services
