PR-54

PR-54 Denial Code: Patient Responsibility

Last Updated: June 2026 | Category: Patient Responsibility

📋 What is PR-54 Denial Code?

PR-54 indicates that patient is responsible for charges where the service exceeds the plan's benefit limit.

🔍 Common Causes of PR-54 Denials

❌

Common Causes

  • Benefit limit exceeded
  • Service count limit reached
  • Frequency limit exceeded

✅ How to Resolve PR-54 Denials

1

Review the Denial

Carefully review the remittance advice to understand the specific denial reason.

  • Bill patient for services beyond the limit
  • Verify the benefit limit with the payer

📞 What to Ask the Payer

Essential Questions for PR-54 Denials:

  1. "What specific documentation is needed to resolve this denial?"
  2. "What is the appeal deadline for this denial?"
  3. "Can this claim be corrected and resubmitted?"
  4. "Is there a retro-authorization or exception process available?"

💡 Prevention Strategies

  • Track benefit utilization
  • Inform patients of limits
