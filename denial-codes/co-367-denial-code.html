CO-367

CO-367 Denial Code: Payment Adjusted

Last Updated: June 2026 | Category: Payment Adjustment

📋 What is CO-367 Denial Code?

CO-367 indicates that payment has been adjusted because the claim was submitted after the allowed filing period.

🔍 Common Causes of CO-367 Denials

❌

Common Causes

  • Claim filed late
  • Filing deadline exceeded
  • Late submission without exception
  • No proof of timely filing

✅ How to Resolve CO-367 Denials

1

Review the Denial

Carefully review the remittance advice to understand the specific denial reason.

  • Gather proof of timely filing
  • Appeal with proof if available
  • Request good cause exception

📞 What to Ask the Payer

Essential Questions for CO-367 Denials:

  1. "What specific documentation is needed to resolve this denial?"
  2. "What is the appeal deadline for this denial?"
  3. "Can this claim be corrected and resubmitted?"
  4. "Is there a retro-authorization or exception process available?"

💡 Prevention Strategies

  • Submit claims promptly
  • Track filing deadlines
  • Maintain proof of submissions
