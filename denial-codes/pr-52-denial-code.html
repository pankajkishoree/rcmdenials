PR-52

PR-52 Denial Code: Patient Responsibility

Last Updated: June 2026 | Category: Patient Responsibility

📋 What is PR-52 Denial Code?

PR-52 indicates that patient is responsible for charges related to a service that was not authorized or referred.

🔍 Common Causes of PR-52 Denials

❌

Common Causes

  • Service not authorized
  • No referral obtained
  • Patient self-referred to specialist

✅ How to Resolve PR-52 Denials

1

Review the Denial

Carefully review the remittance advice to understand the specific denial reason.

  • Bill patient for unauthorized services
  • Obtain retro-authorization if possible

📞 What to Ask the Payer

Essential Questions for PR-52 Denials:

  1. "What specific documentation is needed to resolve this denial?"
  2. "What is the appeal deadline for this denial?"
  3. "Can this claim be corrected and resubmitted?"
  4. "Is there a retro-authorization or exception process available?"

💡 Prevention Strategies

  • Verify authorization requirements
  • Obtain referrals before specialist visits
