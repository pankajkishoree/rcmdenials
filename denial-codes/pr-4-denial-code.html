PR-4: Copayment Amount

Published: June 26, 2026 | Last Modified: June 26, 2026

What is it?

PR-4 is a Patient Responsibility code indicating the patient is responsible for a copayment amount as per their insurance plan.

Root Causes

  • Office visit copay
  • Specialist copay
  • ER copay
  • Procedure copay

Resolution Process

  • Verify copay amount with the payer or insurance card.
  • Clearly explain the copay responsibility to the patient.
  • Bill the patient for the due copayment.
  • Offer convenient payment options or plans if applicable.

What to Ask

Questions to ask internally or the payer to clarify the denial.

  • What is the exact copayment amount indicated by the payer for this service?
  • Is the copayment amount correct based on the patient's benefit plan?
  • Was the copayment collected at the time of service? If not, why?

This means that your insurance plan requires a specific copayment for the services you received. The amount due is [Specific Copayment Amount].

To resolve this, please remit the payment of [Specific Copayment Amount] to our billing department. We offer several payment options, including [mention options like online portal, mail, phone].

If you have any questions about your copayment responsibility or need to discuss payment arrangements, please do not hesitate to contact our billing department at [Phone Number] or [Email Address].

Sincerely,
[Your Clinic/Hospital Name]

Related Codes

This denial code is often related to the following codes:

PR-1, PR-2, PR-3, CO-50

Prevention

Strategies and best practices to prevent future occurrences of this denial.

  • Accurately collect and verify patient copayments at the time of service.
  • Ensure front-desk staff are trained on identifying and collecting correct copays for different service types (office visit, specialist, ER, procedure).
  • Use real-time eligibility tools to confirm copay amounts before providing services.
  • Clearly communicate copay responsibilities to patients during the scheduling or registration process.
