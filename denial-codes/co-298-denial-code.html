CO-298

CO-298 Denial Code: Claim Adjustment

Last Updated: June 2026 | Category: Payment Adjustment

📋 What is CO-298 Denial Code?

CO-298 indicates that this claim has been adjusted per payer rules and guidelines.

🔍 Common Causes of CO-298 Denials

❌

Common Causes

  • Claim does not meet payer guidelines
  • Information required is missing or incorrect
  • Service exceeds allowed limits
  • Payer-specific billing rules apply

✅ How to Resolve CO-298 Denials

1

Step 1

Review the remittance advice carefully

2

Step 2

Correct any identified errors on the claim

3

Step 3

Resubmit within the filing deadline

4

Step 4

Appeal if adjustment is incorrect

📎 Required Documentation for Appeal

  • Original claim with all codes and modifiers
  • Complete medical records supporting medical necessity
  • Any prior authorization or precertification documentation
  • Provider-payer contract or fee schedule reference
  • Detailed appeal letter with clinical justification

💡 Prevention Strategies

  • Stay updated on payer billing rules
  • Use claim scrubbing software before submission
  • Monitor denial trends for patterns

⚖️ Appeal Guidance for CO-298

If you believe this denial was made in error, you have the right to appeal. Follow these steps:

  1. Review the denial thoroughly — Understand exactly why the claim was denied and what documentation is missing.
  2. Gather supporting evidence — Collect all medical records, billing documentation, and any prior authorizations.
  3. Write a detailed appeal letter — Address each denial reason specifically with supporting evidence.
  4. Submit within the deadline — Most payers require appeals within 30-180 days. Check your specific payer's timeline.
  5. Follow up regularly — Track your appeal status and be prepared to provide additional information if requested.

Essential Questions for Your Appeal:

  • "Can you provide the specific contractual clause that applies to this denial?"
  • "What documentation would be sufficient to overturn this denial?"
  • "Is there a peer-to-peer review option available for this type of denial?"
  • "What is the exact appeals process and timeline for this denial type?"
  • "Can the denial reason be clarified in writing with specific reference numbers?"
