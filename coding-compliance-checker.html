Coding Compliance Checker: Catch Errors Before Payer Scrutiny

Coding compliance is the foundation of clean claim submission. Our Coding Compliance Checker validates your diagnosis codes, procedure codes, modifiers, and billing context against CMS National Correct Coding Initiative (NCCI) edits, CPT guidelines, and payer-specific rules — all before the claim reaches the payer.

What Gets Validated

The tool performs a multi-layer compliance check across every element of your claim. ICD-10-CM codes are validated for medical necessity against the billed CPT procedures. CPT codes are checked for correct usage, bundling rules, and modifier requirements. Modifier usage is evaluated against both NCCI edits and payer-specific policies. The tool also considers specialty context, place of service, patient age, and gender to catch age- and gender-specific coding requirements.

Common Compliance Issues We Detect

  • NCCI edit violations — Procedure pairs that cannot be billed together without appropriate modifiers
  • Missing required modifiers — Modifiers needed for specific procedures or payer contracts that were omitted
  • Unbundling violations — Procedures that should be billed under a single comprehensive code
  • Medical necessity gaps — Diagnosis codes that don't support the medical reason for the procedure
  • Age/gender mismatches — Codes used for patient populations they don't apply to
  • Place of service conflicts — POS codes that conflict with the billed procedure or provider type

Why Pre-Submission Compliance Matters

A single compliance error can trigger a denial, delay payment by 60-90 days, and cost $25-50 in rework. For practices billing high-volume procedures, catching these errors before submission means faster payments, fewer appeal letters, and cleaner audit trails. The tool provides specific fix recommendations for each issue found, so your team can correct errors in seconds rather than discovering them weeks later during denial management.

Built for Every Specialty

Whether you're billing for primary care E/M visits, surgical procedures, diagnostic imaging, or physical therapy, the compliance checker applies the correct specialty-specific rules. Select your specialty and payer for the most accurate validation results.

Frequently Asked Questions

What NCCI edits does this tool check?
The tool validates against CMS NCCI edits including Column 1/Column 2 bundling edits, modifier indicators, and medically unlikely edits (MUEs). It covers both professional and facility coding rules.
Can I check multiple diagnosis and procedure codes at once?
Yes. You can enter multiple ICD-10 and CPT codes in a single session. The tool cross-validates all combinations to catch bundling conflicts and medical necessity gaps across your entire claim.
Does this work for Medicare and commercial payers?
Yes. The tool uses CMS NCCI edits as the baseline and applies additional payer-specific rules for major commercial payers. Select your payer for the most accurate compliance results.
How is this different from a clearinghouse edit check?
Clearinghouse edits catch basic formatting and eligibility errors. Our tool goes deeper with medical necessity validation, NCCI bundling analysis, and specialty-specific compliance rules that clearinghouses typically don't check.
Is there a limit to how many codes I can check?
No. There are no usage limits. Run as many compliance checks as needed for your claims without restrictions or account requirements.
