Multi-Claim Denial Tracking and Management Dashboard

Managing dozens or hundreds of denied claims simultaneously requires centralized visibility, consistent status tracking, and efficient prioritization. RCM Denials' Medical Claims Tracker provides a unified dashboard for tracking, sorting, filtering, and managing multiple claims — transforming a chaotic denial queue into an organized resolution workflow.

Claim Data Management

Add claims individually or batch-import from billing systems. Each entry captures claim number, payer, denial code, billed amount, status, and date of service. The tracker provides instant multi-column sorting, real-time filtering by status or payer, and running summaries for total billed, pending, and resolved amounts.

Tracking Capabilities

  • Status management — Update claim status through the complete denial-to-resolution lifecycle
  • Multi-column sorting — Sort by claim number, payer, amount, status, or date of service
  • Advanced filtering — Filter by status category, payer, or free-text search across all fields
  • Real-time summaries — Aggregate totals for claims count, billed amount, and status breakdowns
  • CSV export — Export complete claim datasets for reporting, analysis, or system integration
  • Persistent storage — Browser-based data persistence across sessions for individual workflows

Operational Value

Scattered claim information leads to missed follow-ups, duplicate work, and lost revenue. Centralized tracking enables teams to prioritize by amount, urgency, or payer, ensuring consistent follow-up across the entire denial portfolio. Export to CSV for weekly leadership reporting or use running summaries for real-time performance monitoring.

Team Collaboration

Use the tracker as a shared view during denial management meetings, assign claims to team members, and track progress against resolution targets. The consistent status taxonomy ensures all team members categorize claims uniformly, enabling accurate aggregate reporting.

Frequently Asked Questions

How many claims can I track at once?
There's no limit. You can add as many claims as needed to the tracker. The tool handles batches of 10 or 10,000 with the same performance and filtering capabilities.
Can I export my claim data?
Yes. The Export CSV button downloads your entire claim list as a CSV file that opens in Excel, Google Sheets, or any spreadsheet application. The export includes all columns and current status information.
Does the tracker save my data between sessions?
Yes. Your claim data is saved in your browser's local storage. When you return to the tracker, your claims and statuses are preserved. For team-wide data sharing, export to CSV and import into a shared system.
Can I sort and filter by multiple columns?
Yes. Click any column header to sort by that column (ascending or descending). Use the filter inputs to narrow results by status, payer, or search term. Filters work together so you can combine criteria.
Is this better than using a spreadsheet?
For denial management specifically, yes. The tracker provides built-in status tracking, real-time summaries, and quick filtering that spreadsheets require complex formulas to replicate. It's designed for the specific workflow of managing denied claims, not general data storage.
